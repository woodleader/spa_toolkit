Regulatory Marks

What This Covers

General decision support for CE, WEEE, FCC, and UN ECE R10. This page is tuned for cable assemblies and automotive electronic products and uses conservative "check the route" outputs where vehicle-specific exclusions are often fact-pattern dependent.

Use Boundaries

This helper follows general guidance commonly used in Europe for RoHS/WEEE scope, vehicle-specific exclusions, RED radio products, FCC Part 15, and UN ECE R10 automotive EMC.

  • Generic or dual-use products are treated as normal EEE unless a narrower exclusion clearly applies.
  • Vehicle-specific products get a CHECK result when outcome depends on whether the product is a separately marketed EEE item or an integrated transport/vehicle part.
  • Wireless products stay on the RED/CE path even when used in vehicles.
  • UN ECE R10 is shown as the automotive approval route, not as a substitute for every EU product-law obligation.

Decision Helper

Outcome wording is intentionally strict:

  • REQUIRED means the route is normally expected.
  • CHECK means the route depends on scope or exclusion facts.
  • NO means the mark is generally not expected for the described product form.
